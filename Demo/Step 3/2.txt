Pick any empty folder. Put it anywhere (desktop, O:, wherever).
Put all the TBs for your binder in it.
Open any one of the TBs. Any one.
Click "Generate Mapping Dictionary"
Give it some time to calculate. It will generate a fresh dictionary.
In the future, if you have a dictionary filled out, you can re-use it.
You don't have to generate a new one. Just copy/paste the old one into this folder and update it.
